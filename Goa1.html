Nilgiri Wood Pigeon

The Nilgiri wood pigeon is large pigeon found in the Western Ghats in southwestern India.Its scientific name is Columba elphinstonii. It is endangered due to habbitat distrucion , human setlements ,ect.

The Nilgiri Wood Pigeons diet consists of Wild fruits, berries, seeds, flowers and leaf buds.

Conservation status- Vulnerable


Malabar Pied Hornbill

The Malabar pied hornbill , also known as lesser pied hornbill is a part of the hornbill family. It is scientificaly known as Anthracoceros coronatus. It is endangered due to habbitat loss.

Malabar Pied Hornbills are omnivorous

Conservation status- Near Threatened
