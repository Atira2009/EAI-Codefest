Sambar deer

The Sambar deer is a large deer native to the Indian subcontinent, South China, and Southeast Asia , scientificaly known as Rusa unicolor .It is endangered because of hunting.

The Sambar deer feed on vegetation, including grasses, foliage, browse, fruit, and water plants .

Conservation status- Vulnerable
